Languages By Suklingo



ಕನ್ನಡ (Kannada)

Kannada is a Dravidian language spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is one of the 22 officially recognized languages of India and has a rich literary and cultural heritage. Kannada is the official language of the state of Karnataka and is spoken by the majority of its population. Learning Kannada can be particularly beneficial if you plan to live, work, or travel in Karnataka or other regions where Kannada is spoken.

தமிழ் (Tamil)

Tamil is a Dravidian language with a rich linguistic and cultural heritage spoken predominantly in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

हिन्दी (Hindi)

Hindi is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world and serves as the lingua franca for a significant portion of the Indian subcontinent. It is the official language of the Indian government and one of the 22 scheduled languages of India. As an Indo-Aryan language, Hindi is characterized by its rich literary tradition, encompassing classical texts, modern literature, and a vibrant film industry commonly known as Bollywood.
